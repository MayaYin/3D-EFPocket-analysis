NOTES
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

* Channels with prominent post-stimulation spike activity.

Optimal stimulation response: biphasic, +/-50 uA, 5 ms/phase, 5 pulses, 20 Hz.
Recommended filter: 60 Hz notch, 300-5000 Hz bandpass.
Water splitting observed at highest amplitude (+/-50 uA), delta_V > 1.5 V.
Most stimulations delivered every ~30 s.
